The 4 R's

RECYCLE, REUSE, REDUCE, RECOVER

The 4 R's provides an ecologically sound and environmentally friendly approach to minimizing and managing waste and waste streams.

Applying the 4Rs means consuming intelligently, thus making substantial savings (in terms of money, time, pollution, waste and energy)

The 4 R's
Why reduce?

We live in an era of over-consumption. Everything can be bought. Everything is monetized. Durable products are rare.

And this consumption—goods production, transportation, packaging and waste management—is all too often at the expense of the environment. At the expense of life. At our children's and grandchildren's expense.
